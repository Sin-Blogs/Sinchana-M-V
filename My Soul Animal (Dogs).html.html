My Soul Animal

Dogs

My Soul Animal is a Dog because they are the friendliest animals that I have seen.
Dogs provide companship and company. They can be comfort to people who crave unconditional interaction with another living being. They are termed as 'Man's Best Friend' because can reduce stress, anxiety, ease loneliness, encourage exercise and playfullness, and even improve our cardiovascular health. Caring for an animal can help children grow up more active. Dogs also provide valuable companionship with older adults.






My Favourite Breeds Of Dogs

1. Pomerian

Pomerian are the cutest breed of dogs which I have seen especially the puppies. I only like a white pomerian coz only they look cute. Their fur are soo soft, fluffly and smooth like velvet. And also they are very playful.

2. Golden Retriever

Golden Retriever are one of the most gentle and kind dog breeds. They are playful, yet gentle with children, and they tend to get along well with other pets and strangers. Even their fur is also very soft and fluffy and are known as the most friendlies dog breed.

3. Labrador

Labrador is a loyal and lively , water loving dogs. They make exceptional family member.They are originated in Newfoundland and was brought by a fisherman. The intresting fact about them is they are famous for their love of water. And also they are so cute.

4. Shih Tzu

Shih Tzu is sweet, playful, friendly, alert, curious, busy and cutest dog. They are lively and friendly with people of all ages and with other dogs as well. Rarely you will find a snippy Shih Tzu but most are very sweet. I like their fur a lot, they are very fluffy, smooth and unique.

5. Pug

Pugs are great family dogs and are very playful and affectionate with children. Also, because of thier mouth shape, pugs have trouble delivering an aggressive bite and so are considered a kid-safe breed spectrum. And it is one of the oldest dog breeds.
